Definition, types & challenges of smart accessories

  1. What are smart accessories?
    2. Are any kind of electronic device designed to be worn on the user's body. Such devices can take many different forms, including jewelry, accessories, medical devices, and clothing or elements of clothing.

  2. What are some common types of smart accessories used daily by people?
    1. Smart watch
    2. Smart ring

  3. Common challenges experienced by people
    1. Cyberbullying
    2. Time-management/procrastination
    3. Invasion of privacy

Benefits of smart accessories
  1. Better Healthcare: Wearable smart devices, such as heart and fitness trackers, provide precise, blood pressure monitors, and real-time health data.

  2. Efficiency and Productivity have Increased: Sensors that allow you to monitor valuable resources such as inventory, fuel, and available spare parts.

  3. Improved Control And Convenience: Consider arriving at work and discovering that the office is always at the perfect temperature. Smart thermostats that automatically adjust indoor temperatures through the use of smart watches, make this possible.
